What else do I need?

To run a website you will also need a domain name (ie www.mydomain.com.au) , and somewhere to host it (a hosting provider). We can guide you on how to get both of these.

What are my ongoing costs?

Your domain name is generally purchased annually. While hosting services are subscriptions based (ie monthly or annually). In most cases, these are the only costs. Other costs associated will depend if you need to purchase special plug-ins or services that are specific to your business. We will inform you of these costs based on your requirement.

What if your packages don't address my needs?

We have put the packages to help businesses get started. We can always customise a quote to suit your needs. Please contact us for a free quote

Can I manage my website once complete?

Yes, you will be able to update your website and change text/images…etc. We also include a free session to show you how to edit and update your site, this will help you stay in control.

Do I own the website after you complete it??

Once your website is ready we do a complete handover to you with all admin and password information. We setup the website to be independent of us, this will allow you to choose whether you maintain it yourself or have someone do it for you.
